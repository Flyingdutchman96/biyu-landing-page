Our New Direction

At BIYU, we’re shifting our focus from B2C to B2B, evolving our software into a powerful B2B SaaS solution. Our platform is designed to fully automate asset management for rental and Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS) companies, streamlining operations and boosting efficiency.

As a bootstrapped company, we’re taking this transition one step at a time. Our website will soon reflect these exciting changes, but for now, we’re focused on building the best tools to empower your business.
